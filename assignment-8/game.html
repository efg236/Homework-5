Carrot

They are good for you! Carrots have vitamin A and C in them that helps boost immunity, helping you with fighting off cold symptoms and preventing new ones.

Banana

They are good for you! Bananas are rich in potassium that helps fend off bloat and magnesium that helps fight pain, easing the lack of appetite you feel when sick.

Eggplant

They are actually not great to eat when sick! They contain high levels of histamines that will make your nasal passage swell and make your congestion worse.

Rice

This can be good for you! Rice contains tryptophan, an amino acid that can help induce sleepiness. Congee is a way to go!

Strawberries

They are surprisingly bad for you during a cold! They release histamine that can contribute to congestion. Stay away from them until you get better!

Avocado

Not a good idea! This fatty fruit is high on histamines - eating more of it will make your runny nose worse! Keep the guac for when you are feeling better!

Coffee

Save the morning joe for brighter days! Coffee will only dehydrate your body when it needs water the most. That latte will have to wait.

Pineapple

Great for that stuffy nose! Pinneapple contains bromelain that has been shown to thin mucus. Time to chop down on a few!

Water

Drink plenty of fluids! Being hydrated is your quickest way to recovery!
